Act as a 10+ years experienced full-stack app developer, UI/UX designer, product strategist, and startup founder.

Build a premium, addictive, eye-catching, user-friendly web app called:

MyCarQR

TAGLINE:
“Scan to Reach the Car Owner”

CORE IDEA:
MyCarQR helps car owners create a smart QR code for their vehicle. Anyone can scan the QR code to contact the owner during parking issues, emergencies, blocked vehicles, lights left on, scratches, wrong parking, or urgent situations — without exposing unnecessary personal details.

IMPORTANT:
Build a complete working MVP, not just UI.
Make it mobile-first, premium, smooth, and production-ready.

TECH STACK:
- React
- Tailwind CSS
- Node.js / Express if backend needed
- Supabase or Firebase for auth/database
- QR code generation
- Responsive web app

- Wrong parking

Contact options:
- Call owner
- WhatsApp owner
